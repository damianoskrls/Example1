Welcome to Villa Margarita

Villa Margarita is a family run business of tradionally built rooms and apartments to let in Mykonos island, Greece. It is built to offer you the most enjoyable, relaxing and afordable stay in wonderful Mykonos!

The ideal location, the clean, traditionally decorated rooms and apartments and, above all, the true Mykonian hospitality, make Villa Margarita your best choice for your Greek island escape or even for your honeymoon!

Owners and …’responsible’ for your perfect stay, are Mrs Margarita and Mr Elias Kotsiakis. Along with their three sons, Panos, Nikos and Alexandros, they welcome Villa Margarita’s guests since 1992!

We will be glad to help you ideally organize your holidays in Mykonos island!

So, what are you waiting for? Contact us and prepare your luggage having as destination the sea and the sun, the relaxation and the fun. In an island which is among the world’s best holiday destinations!
